Developments, applications, and validation studies using CompDam have been described in numerous papers. This page provides a bibliography in reverse chronological order. References that include significant description or discussion of the theory underlying CompDam are **bolded**. Conference papers :microphone:, reports :ledger:, and journal papers :page_facing_up: are included. In a few cases, the paper was recognized with a best paper award :1st_place_medal:!
